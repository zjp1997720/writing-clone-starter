# Justin Welsh｜Contrastive Pairs

## 1. 普通强稿会用数字开场；Justin Welsh 更像会给数字 + 排除条件 + 路径翻译

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "I built a 7-figure business from scratch."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "I've run my business since 2019 with zero employees. A clear roadmap helped me past $4M+ in income with no paid ads. Here's the system."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是成就展示
- 后者是成就 + 排除条件 + 可复制路径的三连

## 2. 普通强稿会给编号清单；Justin Welsh 更像会让每条清单带经营判断

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "7 Tips for Better Content Marketing" + 每条解释一个通用建议

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "7 Solopreneur Marketing Strategies" + 每条给一个真实案例和经营取舍（比如："With social media, you're renting. With an email list, you're owning."）

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是信息整理
- 后者每条都在做判断，不是在解释概念

## 3. 普通强稿会说"be authentic"；Justin Welsh 更像会把"独特性"拆成公式

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Be yourself and share your unique perspective."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "In a niche of one, you create one idea, then learn to say it, do it, or package it 1,000+ different ways."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是励志口号
- 后者是可操作的定位工程公式

## 4. 普通强稿会说"克服恐惧"；Justin Welsh 更像会用赔率分析重写恐惧

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Don't let fear hold you back from starting."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Likely worst case is you go back to work. Likely best case is you unlock a better life. Feels riskier never to try."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是情绪鼓励
- 后者是用 worst-case / best-case 的赔率对比让读者用理性做决策

## 5. 普通强稿会总结 3 个要点；Justin Welsh 更像会给一个极简行动指令

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "To recap: 1) Find your niche, 2) Build your content system, 3) Start today."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Your 30-day challenge: Pick one thing to learn, write about it daily, publish it publicly. Start today."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是信息回顾
- 后者是单一可执行动作 + 时间边界

## 6. 普通强稿会用"follow your passion"；Justin Welsh 更像会用"Skills + Interests = Unique combination"

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Follow your passion and the money will follow."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Skills (Excel at) + Interests (Passionate about) = Idea (Unique combination). Find the intersection, then build your niche around it."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是情感建议
- 后者是双变量定位公式

## 7. 普通强稿会讲"how to grow on social media"；Justin Welsh 更像会先指出"内容是商品"的残酷现实

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Here are 5 strategies to grow your social media following."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Most content is a commodity. The only way to win is to bring a unique, non-obvious perspective learned through your personal experience."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是方法教程
- 后者先给出一个大多数人不愿意面对的判断，再从判断里长出方法

## 8. 普通强稿会引用大佬案例；Justin Welsh 更像会叫你别学大佬

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Elon Musk did X, Jeff Bezos did Y. Here's what we can learn."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Stop idolizing outliers like Musk and Bezos. Study the person making $100,000 and follow their lead."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是幸存者偏差的间接传播
- 后者是反共识的落地路径指引

## 9. 普通强稿会说"持续输出就好"；Justin Welsh 更像会把内容系统工程化

### 普通强稿会怎么写

- "Consistency is key. Just keep posting every day."

### Justin Welsh 更像会怎么写

- "Content Matrix: Themes (topics) x Styles (listicle, motivational, contrarian, actionable) = structured content system. And repurpose often: 75% of people didn't see it, 80% didn't remember it, 100% of new followers never saw it."

### 真差异落在哪一层

- 前者是鸡汤式坚持
- 后者是工程化的内容生产 + 分发系统
